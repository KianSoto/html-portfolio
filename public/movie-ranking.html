My Top 3 Animes

Attack On Titan

Is an Anime regarding the last human civilization on Earth that are stuck in 3 walls to protect themselves from the Titans.

Tokyo Ghoul

An Anime about a highschool student who became a ghoul after an encounter with a mysterious woman.

Black Clover

An Anime about a human who grew up without magic in a world of magic.
Instead he had anti-magic which is very powerful.

My Top 3 Movies

Interstellar

A movie about a man who is needed to pilot an aircraft to space to save Earth from its doom.

Fast and Furious

A series of movies regarding racing.

Inception

A movie about reaping time and space and going back to the past.
